About Me

For the last 21 years, I have spent up all of my life living in Mentor, Ohio, a suburb about 30 minutes east of Cleveland. Growing up, I had a huge love of sports, reading, baking, and of course technology. When I wasn't participating in an intense 1 on 1 against my brother on the basketball court outside, or baking chocolate chip cookies with my mom, I was learning about technology with my dad. We talked about firewalls, the internet, how cirtcuits work; I even remember taking apart my electronic toys after I was done with them to look at their circuits. Once I was in my junior year of high school, I knew that I wanted to major in Computer Science. From that moment onward, I spent much of my time learning new languages, technologies, and other facets of my now major. Now, I am less than 6 months away from graudating, and am looking for a company to cement my future at.
